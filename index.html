Reconstitution Instructions

Inspect vials and check expiration dates.

Swab vial tops with alcohol swabs.

Use a sterile syringe to draw the calculated amount of sterile water.

Inject the water into the Semaglutide vial, aiming towards the side to minimize foaming.

Gently swirl to dissolve; do not shake.

Ensure the solution is clear and free of particles.

Draw the reconstituted solution as needed.

Store remaining solution as per instructions.
